Toddler Bite Back and Kill Snake That Bit Her

source: https://www.newsweek.com/toddler-snake-bite-kills-snake-1733553

This incident happened in the village of Kantar, near Bingol, Turkey. A 2-years-old girl was playing in her home's backyard when she started screaming loudly. Her neighbours immediately rushed to check and were shocked to find a bite mark on her lip and a 50cm snake clamped in her mouth.


It is to believe that when the snake bit the toddler, she was frighten and reacted to bit back and cause the snake to be bitten to dead. The species of the snake is remain unknown, but the toddler wasn't seriously harmed because the snake is luckily not a venomous species.
