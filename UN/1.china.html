Man Attempt to Hold Back Flood with QiGong

source: https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1ai4y1V7Pz/

A man in Hunan Province, China, tried to use his 'power' called QiGong to hold back the rolling waves of the flood. According to the video shared, the man dances around, rolling on the ground and performs a series of meditating movement that is believed to be QiGong. QiGong is a Chinese breathing exercise that is often described as a abnormal power in martial novels.


The video spreaded on the Internet and receives critism. Many people online consider the man's action to be disturbing to the local firestations that are busy saving over flood victim.
