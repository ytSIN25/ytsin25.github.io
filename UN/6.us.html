Drug Dealer Calls Police to Report for Stolen Drugs

source: https://www.ndtv.com/offbeat/drug-dealer-calls-cops-to-report-stolen-cocaine-arrested-1729058

Calling police as a criminal might be silly, but this man in Florida still manage to perform such action. On 2 July 2017, 32-years-old David Blackmon called the Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office and describing himself as a drug dealer.


In the phone call, he told the police officer that someone stole about a quarter ounce of cocaine and $50 that was inside his car. However, when the police officer reached the scene he found cocaine in the same spot he described. The man then was arrested and later released on Tuesday with a $4k bond.
