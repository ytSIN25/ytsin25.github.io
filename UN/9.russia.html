Man Sues Apple Claiming iPhone Turned Him Gay then Dropped Lawsuit

source: https://thecoinoffering.com/crypto/gaycoin/

Using a phone might give you an eyes concern, but have you ever heard that it could make you gay? In a suit filed on 20 September,a man claimed a crypto-currency called "GayCoin" was delivered via smartphone app, instead of the Bitcoin he had ordered. The complainant stated: 'How can I judge something without trying? I decided to try same-sex relationships'.


He then added that this make 'his life changed for the worse' and sued Apple that they 'pushed him towards homosexuality through manipulation'. A few days later, he dropped the lawsuit. According to his lawyer, the man withdraw because of privacy fears.
