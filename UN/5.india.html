Man Rapes 65-Year-Old Mother Twice As 'Punishment'

source: https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/delhi-man-rapes-65-year-old-mother-twice-as-punishment-arrested-police-9100465

A 39-years-old man was arrested for raping his 65-years-old mother twice. According to police, the crime happened after the family returned from a trip to Saudi Arabia. The case was reported by the woman accompanied by her 25-year-old daughter in the Hauz Qazi police station.


'The son locked his mother in a room, beat her and threatened her. A few days later he again confined her in a room and raped her, claiming he was 'punishing her' for her past conduct,' a senior officer stated to the news reporter. A case under Section 64 (rape) of BNS has been registered, and the accused has been arrested.
