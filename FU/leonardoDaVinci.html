Saint Jerome in the Wilderness


When it comes to art, most people definitely knows Leonardo Da Vinci. His works such as "The Last Supper" and "Mona Lisa" is priceless and a artwork of all human. However, not all his artwork are all as famous as the Mona Lisa. The painting I will be showing is Saint Jerome in the Wilderness. This painting is an unfinished painting by Leonardo Da Vinci, showing Saint Jerome lived the life of a hermit during his retreat to the Syrian desert. If you want to see the painting by yourself, it is usually display at the Vatican Museums.
