Van Gogh


Wheatfield With Crows


Many people might know the "Starry Night" or "Self Portrait" which are both Van Gogh's art, but this where many stops. Actually, there are also a lot of paintings from Van Gogh that are nice and pretty. The example I choose to give here is the "Wheatfield with Crows". This painting is one of Van Gogh's final paintings one week only before he suicided. Van Gogh's letters suggests that Wheatfield with Crows was completed around 10 July 1890.
