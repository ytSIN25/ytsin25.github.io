The 11th Hour


Jack in the "Titanic" and Jay Gatsby in "The Great Gatsby", Leonardo Di Caprio is truly one of the best actors in the world. Despite all his famous movies, what's not so famous is his documentary series. The 11thHour, is a documentary film created, produced, co-written and narrated by Leonardo DiCaprio. Like his another documentary "Before The Flood", both film discuss about environmental problems and climate change. Many would find documentary films boring, but I really personally recommend all to watch it as it is really a good topic and meaningful.
