Hidetaka Miyazaki


Demon's Souls


When it comes to game developers, many would just know their best selling games. For Hidetaka Miyazaki, his name is as famous as his games. For example, "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice" and also the classic "Elden Ring". His design on traps and monsters make players feel enjoy and annoying at the same time. On year 2009, he designs the game "Demon's Souls" and it was then published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The game is a rpg game, with third-person perspective and can be played on PlayStation3.
