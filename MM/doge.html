What is it?

Doge(slang for dog) is a series of pictures about shiba inus, sometimes edited which different faces and captions. The word 'Doge' came from an episode of Homestar Runner's puppet show as a intentionally misspelled word.

Who is Doge

The picture of Doge that is widely spread is actually a shiba inu named Kabosu owned by a Japanese kindergarten teacher. The most used Doge image is the Doge sitting on a couch and glares sideways which looks funny.
