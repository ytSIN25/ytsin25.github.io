What is it?

The Italian Brainrot is not a meme video or image, but a series of creations by many content creators. The Italian Brainrot features many animals combined together by the generative artificial intelligence to create absurd looking creatures.The video is usually narrated with a male Italian text-to-speech AI voice reading rhythmed names.
